3.1 Nature of AI Features

The App incorporates the following artificial intelligence features powered by third-party large language model infrastructure provided by Anthropic PBC:

  • SAGE AI Assistant: A conversational AI assistant accessible within the App that responds to user queries relating to personal finance, organisation and general information. The text content of queries you submit to SAGE is transmitted to Anthropic's API for processing
