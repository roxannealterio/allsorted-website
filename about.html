Our story

Hi, I’m Roxanne.

I’m a real estate agent and entrepreneur, and like most people running flat out, admin was the thing that always slipped. Work trips I forgot to log. Receipts in the bottom of my bag. Bills paid late. Half a dozen apps that didn’t talk to each other, and a tax return built on guesswork.

I went looking for one app that just handled all of it. It didn’t exist. So I built it.

All Sorted is the app I wished I’d had. It logs my trips while I drive, sorts my receipts and bills, keeps my calendar and lists in order, and shows me exactly what I’m owed at tax time. One place. Nothing slips.

Roxanne Alterio
Founder, All Sorted · Made in Australia
