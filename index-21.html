What's inside

One app. Every tab.

Wallet · Bills · Budgeting · Money · Drive · Life — everything in one place.

Wallet

Gift cards, memberships, discount codes and important documents — all stored securely in one place.

Bills

Track every bill and subscription. Auto-reminders before due dates, ATO deduction flagging and one-tap Pay Now links.

Budgeting

Set a weekly spending plan for fuel, groceries, dining and more. Track savings goals and watch your money grow towards anything that matters.

Tax & ATO

Scan receipts, log deductions from bills automatically and watch your estimated refund grow in real time. Export everything for tax time.

Drive

GPS auto-tracking with movement detection. Get notified when the app senses you are on the move and log work trips at $0.88/km automatically.

Life

Smart notes and reminders that detect dates and bills. Type it once and we'll offer to set a reminder, add it to your calendar or file it automatically.

Money & Records

Track income and expenses, import bank CSV and watch work expenses auto-flag as ATO deductions. Beautiful graphs included.
