Auto-detect logs trips for you

Turn on Auto-Detect in Drive Settings. All Sorted detects movement, starts recording and sends a notification. Stop for 90 seconds and the trip saves automatically with another notification.

AI scans receipts and bills

Tap Scan on Receipts or Bills. AI reads the store, amount, date, category and BPAY code automatically, and pre-ticks Yes if it looks like a work expense. Or paste your bill email and it extracts details on-device.

Share bills and receipts instantly

Both Bills and Receipts have a Share button in the header. Tap it to send a formatted summary via Messages, WhatsApp or email. One tap. No formatting. No manual export.

Invite friends to events

Tap any event and choose Invite. All Sorted finds the person if they are on the app. Send in-app or share a link. The invite lands on their calendar automatically.

Tax refund live on your home screen

Your estimated refund is on the home screen as a widget. Every receipt, bill and kilometre updates it live. Export to your accountant in one tap. Everything backs up to the cloud automatically every 30 minutes so nothing is ever lost.

Health syncs from Apple Health

Steps sync automatically on iPhone. Log water and sleep in one tap. Bar charts show your last 7 days so you can spot patterns across the week.

Team expenses in one place

Your team logs their own trips. You see everything in one view. Share bills, receipts or a full drive logbook with your accountant in one tap from any screen.

Passwords stay on your device

Store wifi passwords, PINs and account logins in Wallet. Encrypted and saved on-device only. Never uploaded, never shared. Everything you reach for, always there.
