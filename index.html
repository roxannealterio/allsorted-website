What is inside

Every feature. One place.

Bills, tax, drive, receipts, calendar, health and wallet. All connected. All in one app. Nothing slips.

01
Drive
GPS Auto-Detect Logbook

Turn on once. All Sorted detects movement and logs your trip at $0.92 per km automatically. Works in the background even when the app is closed. Notification when it starts, notification when it saves. Invite your team and their km flow to you.

02
AI
SAGE AI Assistant

Tap the ✦ button anywhere in the app. Ask SAGE anything about your finances. How much tax you could get back, what bills are due, how many km this month. SAGE reads your real data and gives specific answers.

03
Tax
AI Receipt Scanning

Point your camera at any receipt and AI reads the store name, amount, date and category. Tap Yes if it is a work expense and it lands in your ATO deductions instantly. No typing, no guessing.

04
Bills
Bills and BPAY

Every bill tracked with due dates and BPAY built in. Scan a bill and AI fills the details. Paste your bill email and it reads the amount, due date and reference. Notified at 9am on the day a bill is due.

05
Life
Calendar and Life Organiser

Your full calendar in strip, week and month views. Invite friends to events in the app. To do list and grocery list below. Share either list with one tap. Incomplete tasks carry over until you tick them off.

06
Health
Health and Wellness

Three rings track water, steps and sleep. Steps sync from Apple Health. Log any activity with one tap. Seven-day bar charts for every metric so you can spot patterns across the week.

07
Wallet
Wallet

Gift cards, loyalty memberships, discount codes, documents and passwords. Passwords are stored on device only using iOS encryption and never uploaded anywhere. Balances and expiry dates tracked.

08
Tax
Live Tax Refund

Your estimated refund on the home screen updates every time you log a receipt, trip or work bill. Set your tax bracket in Settings and the number is personalised to you. Export to your accountant in one tap.

09
Themes
Four Themes and Cloud Backup

Classic, Blush, Denim and Sage. Your chosen theme is remembered with no colour flash on startup. Automatic cloud backup keeps everything safe. Your data is encrypted and only you can see it.

10
AI
AI Bill Scanning

Photograph any bill and AI reads the biller name, amount, due date and reference number. One tap adds it to your upcoming bills with BPAY pre-filled. Tick ATO Deduction and it flows to your tax total.
