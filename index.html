What’s inside

Seven tabs. One sorted life.

Every feature is connected. A bill flagged links to your ATO. A trip ends and the deduction appears instantly. Log a to do and it lands in your calendar. One app, everything updates.

01
Wallet
Everything you carry, digital

Gift cards with balances. Memberships. Discount codes. Loyalty numbers. Passwords stored on your device only, never uploaded to any server. Everything you reach for, always there.

02
Bills
Pay with BPAY, never miss a payment

Add your biller code and reference number for any bill and pay via BPAY directly from the app. Categories include Car, Invoice, Insurance, Subscriptions and more. Due date reminders. ATO flagging in one tap.

03
Money
Receipts, tax deductions and budgeting

Scan receipts with your camera. Work expenses automatically flagged as ATO deductions. Your full receipt and tax history in one place. Export everything at tax time in seconds.

04
Drive
GPS logbook. Deduction added instantly.

Auto-detect starts your trip when you move. Stop, and $0.88/km lands in your ATO tab. No manual entry. Ever. Your logbook is always complete for tax time.

05
Calendar
Calendar with invites to friends

Strip, week and month views. Add events with colour coding across eight colours. Invite friends by email All Sorted checks if they are on the app and sends an in-app invite, or shares a download link if they are not.

06
Life
Health, Wellness and Daily in one place

Three tabs. Health tracks your water, steps and sleep rings with daily targets. Wellness covers mood check-in, reflect, meditate and manifest. Daily has your to dos, journal and grocery list all in one place.

07
Home
Your life at a glance, your way

Customise your home screen with widgets. Tick off a task and it stays crossed out all day so you can see everything you got done. Four colour themes. Bills, calendar, wellness and more. Your layout, your way.

+
Morning briefing
A PA who knows your finances

Every morning at 7am, All Sorted sends a personalised notification with bills due today, your schedule and a daily quote. Start every day knowing exactly where you stand.

Everything connected. Nothing missed.

Every part of All Sorted links to every other part. Flag a bill as a work expense and it appears in your ATO tab instantly. Finish a drive and the deduction is already logged. To dos carry over until done. Launching June 2026, right before tax time.

To dos that carry over

Incomplete tasks carry forward every day until you tick them off. Done tasks stay crossed out all day so you can see what you got done, then clear overnight. Nothing slips through.

BPAY built in

Add your biller code and reference when you save a bill. Tap BPAY when it is due and the details are right there. No switching apps, no hunting for numbers.

Health connected

Grocery list in your Daily tab
