Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026  ·  All Sorted App Pty Ltd

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. Your passwords never leave your device. You can delete everything at any time.

Who we are

All Sorted App Pty Ltd operates the All Sorted iOS application and the website at allsortedapp.net. In this policy, "we", "our" and "us" refers to All Sorted App Pty Ltd.

If you have questions about this policy, email us at privacy@allsortedapp.net.

What we collect

Information you give us

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Financial data you enter — bills, expenses, income, trips, deductions
  • Profile settings — currency, timezone, tax bracket, notification preferences
  • Savings goals, weekly budgets and account names
  • Notes and reminders you create

Information collected automatically

  • GPS location data when you use the Drive feature and have granted permission
  • Basic device information for troubleshooting (device type, iOS version)
  • App usage patterns to improve features (no personally identifiable data)

What we never collect

  • Passwords and PINs stored in the Wallet tab — these are stored on your device only and never transmitted to our servers
  • Your bank account credentials — we use CSV import only
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Data from other apps on your device

How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the All Sorted app
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To send notifications you have requested (bill reminders, morning briefings)
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns
  • To send you product updates and launch information if you have joined the waitlist

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling or sale to third parties.

How we store your data

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, located in Australia. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest. We use row-level security so that only your account can access your data.

Passwords, PINs and login details stored in the Wallet tab are stored on your device only using iOS secure storage. They are never transmitted to our servers.

Who we share data with

We share your data with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider
  • Apple — for in-app purchases and notifications via iOS
  • Formspree — for waitlist email collection on our website

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to any third party. We do not share your data with advertisers.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data at any time using the export features in the app
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@allsortedapp.net.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete all your data within 30 days. Backups are purged within 90 days.

Children

All Sorted is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
