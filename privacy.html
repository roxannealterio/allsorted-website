Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026  ·  All Sorted App Pty Ltd (ABN on file)  ·  Queensland, Australia

Summary: All Sorted App Pty Ltd collects only the personal information necessary to provide the All Sorted application and related services. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties. Passwords and PINs stored within the Wallet feature are retained exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. You retain the right to access, correct and delete your data at any time.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how All Sorted App Pty Ltd ("All Sorted", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, discloses and manages personal information in connection with the All Sorted iOS application (the "App") and our website at allsortedapp.net (the "Website").

This Policy is prepared in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By accessing or using the App or Website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

Questions or complaints regarding this Policy should be directed to: info@allsortedapp.net

2. What Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Name and email address upon account registration
  • Financial records entered by you, including bills, income, expenses, savings goals and ATO deduction data
  • Profile preferences including currency, timezone, tax bracket and notification settings
  • Notes, reminders and wellness entries you create within the App

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • GPS location data when you use the Drive feature, subject to your express permission via iOS location services
  • Device information including device type and iOS version, used solely for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes
  • Aggregated, de-identified usage data used to analyse and improve App features

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • Passwords, PINs and login credentials stored in the Wallet tab. These are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • Bank account credentials. The App uses CSV import only and does not connect directly to banking institutions
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data
  • Data from other applications on your device

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account and provide the services described in the App
  • To synchronise your data across your authorised devices
  • To deliver notifications you have enabled, including bill reminders and morning briefings
  • To process subscription payments through Apple's in-app purchase system
  • To respond to support enquiries
  • To send product updates, launch communications and waitlist notifications where you have opted in
  • To improve the App using aggregated, de-identified analytics that cannot identify you personally

We will not use your personal information for any purpose that is incompatible with the purposes listed above without first obtaining your consent.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We disclose personal information only to the following third parties, each of whom is engaged solely to assist in the delivery of the App:

  • Supabase Inc. , database hosting and authentication infrastructure, operated from servers located in Australia
  • Apple Inc. , in-app purchase processing and push notification delivery via APNs
  • Formspree Inc. , waitlist email collection via our Website

We do not disclose personal information to advertisers, data brokers or any other third party for commercial purposes. We do not sell personal information.

We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law, by court order, or by a regulatory authority having jurisdiction over All Sorted.

5. Storage and Security

Your personal information is stored on Supabase-managed servers located in Australia. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher. All data at rest is encrypted. Access controls include row-level security, ensuring that only your authenticated account can retrieve your data.

While we implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is entirely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law

Subject to the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate, incomplete or out-of-date information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated personal information
  • Export your personal data using the export functionality within the App
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time by unsubscribing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au if you believe your privacy rights have been interfered with

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@allsortedapp.net. We will respond within a reasonable time and, in any event, within 30 days of receipt.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active or as required to provide the services. Upon account deletion, personal data is permanently removed from our primary systems within 30 days and from backup systems within 90 days of the deletion request.

8. Children

The App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you have reason to believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@allsortedapp.net and we will take prompt steps to delete that information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Where changes are material, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notification prior to the changes taking effect. The effective date at the top of this Policy will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App following notification of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
