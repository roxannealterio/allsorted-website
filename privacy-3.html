Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026 · All Sorted App Pty Ltd

The short version: We only collect what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. Your passwords never leave your device. You can delete everything at any time. That is it.

Who we are

All Sorted App Pty Ltd runs the All Sorted iOS app and the website allsortedapp.net. When we say "we" or "us" in this document, that is who we mean.

Questions? Email us at info@allsortedapp.net.

What we collect

We collect your name and email when you sign up. Inside the app, we store the financial information you enter yourself, like bills, expenses, work trips and savings goals. We also store your settings and preferences.

When you use the Drive feature, we access your GPS location to log your trip. We only do this when you have given permission and the feature is active.

We do not collect your passwords or PINs stored in the Wallet tab. Those stay on your device only and are never sent to our servers.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, data from other apps, or your bank credentials. We use CSV import only, never direct bank access.

How we use it

We use your data to run the app, sync it across your devices, and send you the notifications you have asked for like bill reminders and your morning briefing. We also use anonymised and aggregated data to improve features, but this cannot identify you personally.

If you joined our waitlist, we use your email to send you launch updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

We do not use your financial data for advertising. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Where we store it

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, based in Australia. All data is encrypted when it travels between your phone and our servers, and encrypted when stored. Only your account can access your data.

Who we share it with

We work with a small number of trusted services to run the app:

  • Supabase, for our database and login system
  • Apple, for in-app purchases and push notifications
  • Formspree, for our website waitlist form

That is the full list. We do not share your data with advertisers or any other third party.

Your rights

You can access, correct or delete your data at any time. You can export everything from inside the app. To delete your account completely, email us and we will do it within 30 days.

You can also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.

Kids

All Sorted is not for children under 13. If you think a child has signed up, contact us and we will remove their data immediately.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes we will let you know by email or in the app. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
