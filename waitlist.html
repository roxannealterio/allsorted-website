What's inside

Six apps worth of features,
in one.

Drive Auto-Detect

Turn it on once in Settings. All Sorted watches for movement and logs your trips automatically at $0.92 per km. Notification when it starts, notification when it saves. ATO logbook always ready.

AI Receipt Scanning

Point your camera at any receipt. AI reads the store name, amount, date and category. Tap Yes if it is a work expense and it lands in your ATO deductions instantly. No typing.

Bills and BPAY

Scan a bill and AI fills the details. BPAY is built in. Notified at 9am on the due date. Tick ATO Deduction on any work bill and it flows to your tax total automatically.

Live Tax Refund

Your estimated refund updates on the home screen every time you log a receipt, trip or work bill. Set your tax bracket and the number is personalised to you.

Calendar and Life

Full calendar in strip, week and month views. To do list, grocery list and meal planner below it. Invite friends to events in the app. Share lists with one tap.

SAGE AI Assistant

Tap ✦ anywhere in the app. SAGE reads your real data and trips, bills, deductions, calendar and and answers specific questions about your actual finances, not generic advice.
