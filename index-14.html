What’s inside

Everything connected. One sorted life.

Every feature talks to every other. Mark a receipt as a work expense and it lands in your tax total instantly. Stop a drive and $0.92/km is logged automatically. Flag a bill as ATO deductible and it flows through. One app, everything updates.

01
Life & Calendar
Your full calendar lives inside Life

Strip, week and month views. Tap any day to add an event with colour, location and video call details. Invite friends by email directly in-app. Sync to iPhone or Google Calendar. Your to do list and grocery list sit right below your calendar so you see your whole day at once.

02
Bills
BPAY, scan or import from email

Add a biller code and pay via BPAY in one tap. Scan a bill with your camera and details fill automatically. Or paste your bill email and All Sorted extracts the amount, due date and reference on-device. Tick ATO Deduction and it flows straight to your tax total.

03
Receipts
Scan, tap Yes and it is in your tax

Scan a receipt and All Sorted reads the store, date and amount automatically. A single Yes/No asks if it is a work expense. Say Yes and it lands in your ATO total instantly. Spending chart by category so you always know where the money went.

04
Drive
The GPS logbook that does everything

Turn on Auto-Detect and every work trip logs itself. Stop moving, and $0.92/km for FY 2025–26 goes straight to your ATO total before you even put your keys down. Three tabs: Logbook shows every trip with route map, from and to addresses, purpose and deduction. Reports shows your financial year summary, monthly breakdown and ATO Logbook Compliance check. Team lets small business owners track multiple drivers from one place. Categories: Client Visit, Site Visit, Supply Run, Conference, Training and more. Export the full logbook as a CSV for your accountant. No manual entry. Ever.

05
Health
Rings, streaks and Apple Health sync

Three rings for water, steps and sleep. Steps sync automatically from Apple Health on iPhone. Log activity with a tap. Bar charts show your last 7 days so you can spot patterns. Daily targets with progress bars and health tips.

06
Tax
Your refund estimate, always visible

Your estimated tax refund lives on the home screen as a clean widget showing the number, a progress bar and deduction count. Tap it for the full breakdown. Receipts, work bills and kilometres all feed into one running total. Export everything for your accountant in one tap.

07
Home
Your life at a glance, your way

Customise with widgets: tax refund, bills due, health rings, grocery list, events, to dos and more. Tick off a task and it stays crossed out all day so you can see what you achieved. Four colour themes. Everything updates live as you log things throughout the day.

08
Wallet
Everything you carry, digital

Gift cards, memberships, discount codes and passwords. Passwords are stored on your device only and never uploaded. Everything you reach for, always there and never lost.
